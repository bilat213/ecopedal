about us

At ECOPEDAL, we are driven by the belief that innovation and sustainability can work hand-in-hand to create practical solutions for the modern world. Our flagship product, the Crank-Powered Bicycle Charger with Dual Functionality, is designed to provide cyclists, delivery riders, and outdoor enthusiasts with a reliable, eco-friendly power source. Combining the energy of pedaling with cutting-edge technology, ECOPEDAL generates electricity on-the-go, ensuring users have both lighting and power for their devices without relying on traditional power outlets.


Founded by a diverse and passionate team of students, ECOPEDAL seeks to bridge the gap between energy independence and environmental responsibility. Whether you’re commuting through the city, exploring remote areas, or navigating challenging conditions during a long ride, our product offers a convenient, portable, and green alternative to conventional charging solutions. By turning kinetic energy into usable power, we aim to empower users, reduce reliance on disposable batteries, and promote a sustainable lifestyle for all.
