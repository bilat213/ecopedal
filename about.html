about us

EcoPedal / about

At ECOPEDAL, we are passionate about creating innovative, sustainable solutions for cyclists and outdoor enthusiasts. Our flagship product, the crank-powered bicycle charger, is designed to harness the kinetic energy generated from pedaling to provide a portable and eco-friendly power source. Whether you're commuting through the city, exploring remote trails, or in need of emergency charging, ECOPEDAL ensures you stay powered up without relying on traditional outlets. We believe in the power of mobility and sustainability, offering a practical, green alternative to conventional charging methods, and making it easier for individuals to stay connected while reducing their environmental impact.
